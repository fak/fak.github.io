Felix A Krüger


Welcome to my homepage! I am a PhD student at the European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI) and graduate member of Fitzwilliam College at the University of Cambridge.

My project is set within the ChEMBL group with John Overington as my thesis advisor. ChEMBL is an open source data base providing bioactivity data for small, drug-like molecules. We also keep a group blog to which I occasionally contribute posts about newly approved drugs.

For my research, I am interested in small molecules that can be used as chemical probes in the life sciences. I am integrating data from ChEMBL and other relevant sources to infer properties of such compounds and to map the target space in reach of current chemical probes. Please feel free to contact me if you have a request or ideas for a collaboration on chemical probes.
